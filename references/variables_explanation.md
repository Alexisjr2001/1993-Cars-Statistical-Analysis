Based on the "Variable_Information_93Cars.pdf" document provided, here is a breakdown of the variables in the dataset, organized by category to help you understand them better.

### **1. Categorical & Coded Variables**

These variables use numerical codes to represent specific categories. You will need to map these numbers to their meanings for analysis:

-   **AirBags:** Indicates the standard airbag configuration.

-   `0` = None

-   `1` = Driver only

-   `2` = Driver & Passenger

-   **Drive Train Type:** Specifies which wheels drive the car.

-   `0` = Rear wheel drive

-   `1` = Front wheel drive

-   `2` = All wheel drive

-   **ManualTrans:** Indicates if a manual transmission version is available.

-   `0` = No

-   `1` = Yes

-   **Domestic:** Origin of the manufacturer.

-   `0` = Non-U.S. manufacturer

-   `1` = U.S. manufacturer

### **2. Pricing Variables**

Prices are recorded in **thousands of dollars (\$1,000s)**:

-   **MinimumPrice:** Price for the basic version of the model.

-   **MaximumPrice:** Price for the premium version of the model.

-   **MidrangePrice:** The average of the Minimum and Maximum prices.

### **3. Engine & Performance**

-   **CityMPG:** Miles per gallon rating for city driving (EPA rating).

-   **HighwayMPG:** Miles per gallon rating for highway driving.

-   **TotalAvgMPG:** Combined average of City and Highway MPG.

-   **EngineSize:** Engine displacement in **liters**.

-   **Horsepower:** Maximum horsepower output.

-   **RPM:** Revolutions per minute at maximum horsepower.

-   **ERM:** Engine revolutions per mile in the highest gear.

-   **NumberOfCylinders:** The count of engine cylinders.

-   **Fuel TankCapacity:** Capacity in **gallons**.

### **4. Dimensions & Capacity**

-   **Passengers:** Passenger seating capacity (persons).

-   **Weight:** Vehicle weight in **pounds**.

-   **Length:** Vehicle length in **inches**.

-   **Width:** Vehicle width in **inches**.

-   **WheelBase:** Distance between front and rear axles in **inches**.

-   **U_turn:** Space required for a U-turn in **feet**.

-   **Rear_seat:** Rear seat room in **inches**.

-   **LuggageCapa:** Luggage capacity in **cubic feet**.

### **5. Identification**

-   **Manufacturer:** The brand of the car (e.g., Ford, Honda).

-   **Model:** The specific model name.

-   **Type:** The classification of the vehicle (e.g., Small, Midsize).
